What if we don't have internet at home?

  1. Try contacting your school. They may have mobile wifi devices available for checkout.
  2. Some schools may have amplified their wifi signal to be available in the school parking lot, check with your local district.
  3. Many public libraries also have mobile wifi devices available for checkout.
  4. Public libraries also have free wifi inside and may have boosted their signals to their parking lots or they may have computers available for use inside.
  5. Some cable and internet providers are offering low cost plans to help families secure wifi for their students learning at home.

What is an LMS?

An LMS is a place where a school can place content, grade student work, communicate with students and parents, and organize and store curriculum and information. It has the advantage of being the same for the entire school so that all students will access their classes and coursework in the same way, easing confusion for students and families.
