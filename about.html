Distance learning during COVID-19

We are a group of educators pursuing the problem of distance learning in the COVID-19 pandemic


We are a group of educational professionals pursuing the problem of distance learning during the COVID-19 pandemic through strategic collaboration. While simultaneously completing our degrees in The Master of Library and Information Science program at Wayne State University.

The purpose of this website stems from the collective objective of completing our final project for INF 7420 Website Development, a class required for all of our programs.

Located in the heart of Detroit, Wayne State offers over 300 academic programs. Of those programs the School of Information Sciences is one of the best known. The Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) degree is an accredited degree that focuses on preparing professionals for leadership roles in libraries and other information organizations.
